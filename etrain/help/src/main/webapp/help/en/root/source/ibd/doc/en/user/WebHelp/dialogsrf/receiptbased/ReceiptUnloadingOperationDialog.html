RF-Terminal Unloading

Purpose

These screens cover the user interface for Quantity and Receipt based Receiving using a RF-Terminal.

      
Functions
  • Quantity based receiving in automated or manual Areas ordered to given and selected Receipt
  • Final storage of Load Units of goods receipt
  • Support of GS1 barcodes
  • In more detail
  • Selection of a Dock and Shipment
  • Identification of the product and the Load Unit
  • Receiving of a certain quantity of the product

For a detailed description, see "Unloading with RF-Terminal".

      
Overview

Dialog Step

Description

Scan Dock

Initial step of the screen. The operator is asked to scan the Id of the Dock for assignment to the upcoming unloading operation.

Scan Shipment

Step after a Dock has been scanned. The operator is asked to scan the Id of the Shipment that he will unload.

Scan Load Unit

Step after a Shipment has been selected. A Load Unit to be unloaded has to be scanned by the operator.

Check LU Type

Step after a Load Unit has been scanned. The Load Unit Type could be initially set, changed or adopted.

Identify Product

Step after a Load Unit and Type has been defined and no Receipt could be determined uniquely. The operator scans a GS1 product barcode for selection of the Receipt.

Check Product

Confirmation of the received quantity of the determined product with booking against the identified Receipt.
