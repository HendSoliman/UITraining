Replenishment

Overview

Replenishment covers stock relocation in the warehouse to support picking and delivery; having the required quantity of the required stock accessible at the optimal Location. There are three kind of Replenishment processes:

  • Threshold-based standard Replenishment - based on defined threshold levels on actual Inventory per SKU and specific Areas.
  • Lookahead Replenishment - estimating the future demand in the Replenishment Destination Disposition Zone, based on pending Orders. (see Lookahead Calculation)
  • Demand based Replenishment - listening for an individual replenishment request by other business processes.

 INFO 

For configuration purposes, see the related topic in the Warehouse Administration Guide.

Replenishment manages business processes for Replenishment administration and maintenance. The fulfillment of Replenishments occurs through Replenishment Orders managed by the subsystem Order Processing (ORP).

Main responsibilities of the component Replenishment:

  • Providing Replenishment configurations for Threshold-based and Lookahead Replenishment.
  • Providing recurring checks for Replenishment needs based on batches or demons for Threshold-based and Lookahead Replenishments
  • Initiating Replenishment execution processes by use of Order Planning and execution subsystems.

Section

Description

Replenishment Supervised Zones

The Replenishment Supervised Zone defines all replenishment specific configuration for a certain Disposition Zone. The definition of a Replenishment Supervised Zone is as well required for the configuration of Replenishment Thresholds.
Replenishment Thresholds

A Replenishment Threshold defines SKU specific triggers and configuration for the creation of replenishment orders of the replenishment types "fix quantity", "fix quantity effective", "total quantity" and "total number of load units".
