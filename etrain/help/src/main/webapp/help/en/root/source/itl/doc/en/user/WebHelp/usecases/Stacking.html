Restacking

Purpose

The Restacking screen supports spontaneous stacking of whole Load Units onto other Load Units and De-stacking. After a Source Load Unit scan, all stacked Load Units on this Load Unit display. With a Destination Load Unit scan, the subsequent booking destination is defined. The Load Unit or Insert is booked onto the Destination Load Unit or directly on the Workstation's location.

      
Prerequisites
  • Screen must be executed at a configured Workstation
  • Source and Destination Load Unit must be available at the Workstation.
