Fields

Field

Description

Disallow Roundup

This flag controls whether or not replenishment quantities are rounded to full layers.

Auto Depalletize

When this flag is set, the packaging variant can be de-palletized automatically.

Layer pattern and pallet structure must conform to requirements for automatic de-palletizing

Shrinked

This flag indicates that the packaging variant is surrounded with shrinked foil.

Box with Cover

Indicates that the packaging has a loose cover which is not tightly bound to the box.

Nesting Stack

Indicates that the packaging variant can be nested.

Stacking Lugs

Indicates that the packaging variant has stacking lugs which stand out at the top of the packaging.

Volume Object

Indicates that the packaging type is cannot be normally palletized but must be added at the top of the pallet

Preferred Low Range

Indicates that the packaging variant shall be placed at the bottom or in the inner area of a pallet where it is not easily accessible.
