SKU ID

SKU of the Aggregated Inventory within the Client. 

Description

Description of the SKU.

Client

Client of the Aggregated Inventory.

Batch of the Aggregated Inventory, if the batch is inventory separating according to the SKU blueprint.

Best before date of the Aggregated Inventory, if the best before date is inventory separating according to the SKU blueprint.

Packaging Level

Packaging Level, if this is inventory separating according to the SKU blueprint.

Overview

Dispo Zone ID

Disposition Zone of this Aggregated Inventory, if the Level of this Aggregated Inventory is "Dispo Zone".

Quantity
Quantity

Total quantity of this Aggregated Inventory expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Unit

Unit of the quantity of the SKU.
Quantity Free

Quantity available for disposition, provided the inventory is not on hold. It covers the total quantity free of the represented Inventory Units, which have the availability status available or reserved. Quantity is expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Quantity Reserved

Part of the represented quantity, which is reserved. Inventory Reservations as well as Inventory Unit Reservations are considered by Quantity Reserved. Quantity is expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Quantity in Receiving

Quantity that is in receiving. Inventory was received, but the Receiving process is not yet finished and not yet confirmed to the host (if inventory owned by the host). Quantity is expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Quantity On Hold

Sum of the quantities of the represented Inventory Units, which are on hold. Quantity expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Quantity Leaving

Part of the quantity, which is already booked onto an Order. This quantity is about to leave the LMS controlled warehouse. Covers all Inventory Units in or past availability status 'picked'. Quantity is expressed in the Base Quantity Unit of the SKU.
Quantity Ordered

Quantity expected as replenishment for the LMS controlled warehouse. Often 0 (zero), because it is not exactly known to LMS. Quantity is expressed in the base quantity unit of the SKU.
Total Weight

Sum of the total weights of the represented Inventory Units.
Net Weight

Sum of the net weights of the represented Inventory Units.

Inventory Reservations

If active, an Inventory Unit Reservation exists for this Inventory Unit.

Fifo Date

FIFO date of the Aggregated Inventory, if the FIFO date is inventory separating according to the SKU blueprint.

Quality Classification
