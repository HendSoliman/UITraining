Pick Operation

Description

Pick operations include the standard case for confirmation of the single pick:

  • without variation
  • with handling of inventory shortage
  • with additional split Pick operation
  • with advised overdelivery
  • with reverse Picking functionality.
      
Triggers and Actors

The following users must perform this after a Source Load Unit scan:

  • Picker at Goods-to-Person Pickstation
      
Prerequisites
  • All required Pick Containers are put on the correct Put Locations.
  • Required Source Load Unit with finished transport to inbound buffer is scanned.
  • Previous Pick is confirmed and further Pick on same Source Load Unit is available.
  • "Reverse Pick recommended" display requires the appropriate Pick Zone configuration (see Detail Dialog Pick Zone Configuration).
      
Work Flow

"OK": standard case

Step

Description

1.

Open the Picking Screen and create a New Pick Container.

2.

"Picking" displays.

3.

Pick the required quantity from Source Load Unit. Put it into the Pick Container at the required Put Location.

4.

Press "Enter" or click "OK".

"Short"

Step

Description

1.

Open the Picking Screen and create a New Pick Container.

2.

"Picking" displays

3.

Not enough inventory is available on Source Load Unit.
Press "F2" or click "F2: Short".

4.

"Short" displays.

5.

Pick the remaining quantity from Source Load Unit. Put it into the Pick Container at the required Put Location.

6.

Enter the picked quantity.

7.

Press "Enter" or click "OK".

Change Function Reason

7a.1

Press "F9" or click "F9: Change Function Reason".

7a.2

Select a Function Reason and press "Enter" or click "OK". Or discard changes: "Esc" / "Cancel".

7a.3

The selected Function Reason displays for later confirmation. Proceed with original step.

Cancel

7b.

If a miscount and enough inventory is available, press "Esc" or click "Cancel".

"Over"

Step

Description

1.

Open the Picking Screen and create a New Pick Container.

2.

"Picking" displays.

3.

Picker desires to pick more than requested quantity.
Press "F3" or click "F3: Over".

4.

"Over" displays.

5.

Pick the desired quantity from Source Load Unit. Put it into the Pick Container at the required Put Location.

6.

Enter the picked quantity.

7.

Press "Enter" or click "OK".

Change Function Reason

7a.1

Press "F9" or click "F9: Change Function Reason".

7a.2

Select a Function Reason and and press "Enter" or click "OK". Or discard changes: "Esc" / "Cancel".

7a.3

The selected Function Reason displays for later confirmation. Proceed with original step.

Cancel

7b.

If a mistake and picking the exact quantity is possible, press "Esc" or click "Cancel".

"Full"

Step

Description

1.

Open the Picking Screen and create a New Pick Container.

2.

"Picking" displays.

3.

The Pick Container is full and not all required quantity fits into the current Pick Container.
Press "F6" or click "F6: Full".

4.

"Full" displays.

5.

Pick less than required quantity from Source Load Unit. Put it into the Pick Container at the required Put Location.

6.

Enter the picked quantity.

7.

Press "Enter" or click "OK".

Change Function Reason

7a.1

Press "F9" or click "F9: Change Function Reason".

7a.2

Select a Function Reason. Confirm with "Enter" or "OK".  Or discard changes: "Esc" / "Cancel".

7a.3

The selected Function Reason displays for later confirmation. Proceed with original step.

Cancel

7b.

If a mistake and all required quantity fits into the Pick Container, press "Esc" or click "Cancel".

"Reverse"

Step

Description

1.

Open the Picking Screen and create a New Pick Container.

2.

"Picking" displays.

3.

Perform a reverse picking operation with exchange of Source Load Unit and Pick Container. E.g. reverse picking is recommended by the system.
Press "F7" or click "F7: Reverse.

4.

"Reverse" displays.

5.

Leave the required quantity on Source Load Unit. Pick the remaining quantity into empty an Pick Container at the required Put Location.

Note: Empty the Pick Container, if needed.

6.

Enter the picked and thus, reverse quantity.

7.

Press "Enter" or click "OK".

8.

Perform the exchange of Source Load Unit and Pick Container (manually or automated).

Shortage

5a.1

Insufficient inventory available on Source Load Unit.
Press "F2" or click "F2: Short".

5a.2

Perform "Short" as outlined in step 4.

5a.3

Proceed with step 8.

Cancel

7b.

If a mistake and reverse picking is currently impossible, press "Esc" or  click "Cancel".
     
Result

Typically, the Pick proceeds to "confirmed" status and the related Inventory Unit is booked into the Pick Container.

Later, if "Short", only the remaining quantity is booked into the Container. The missing quantity is booked related to its deletion and the shortage of related Pick Orders are handled based on the configuration.

Later, if "Full", the Pick is split and a new Pick is available for picking the remaining quantity into a new Pick Container.

Later, if "Reverse", the system books an additional exchange of Source Load Unit and Pick Container.
