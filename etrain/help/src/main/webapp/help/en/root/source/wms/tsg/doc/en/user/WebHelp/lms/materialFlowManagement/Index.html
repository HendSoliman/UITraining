Material Flow Management Errors

Introduction

The Material Flow Management (MFM) component is responsible for administration and manipulation of Transport Requests and their delegation to underlying transport subsystems for execution as well as the management of de-palletizing processes. An overview of the Material Flow Processes are given in Material Flow Management.

Within this section the main exceptional situations of the overall Material Flow Processes of the LMS are described and hints for troubleshooting are provided.

Chapter

Description
Why Not? This topic explains a general approach to find out why a Load Unit does not move in the way it is supposed to. Along with the analysis of the issue the typical fault recovery actions are mentioned.
Restart of an Inbound Transport This topic explains the clarification handling of Load Units with a currently active inbound transport which did not match any "standard transport error" like e.g. "bin occupied". In general this is how to restart the transport (from the Clarification Workstation) and how to reroute the Load Unit in case of required.
