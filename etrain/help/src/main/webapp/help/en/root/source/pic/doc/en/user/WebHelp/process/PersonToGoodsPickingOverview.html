Overview Route Based Picking

Purpose

Route Based Picking is supported through Person-to-Goods picking processes. Person-to-Goods Picking deals with all picking processes special to manually operated areas where Pickers are guided to storage locations for pick operations of the goods from the Pick Face into manually transported Pick Containers.
Person-to-Goods Picking can be done with online or offline picking modes. The online picking mode is supported by guiding the Picker with a web based RF-Terminal and direct online pick confirmation. Offline picking is paper-based with pick confirmation for entire Pick Walks done by screens.

 INFO
