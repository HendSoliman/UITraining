Shipping

Overview

Shipping provides the components to support various execution processes for the shipment of orders.

Routes

A Route is the central business object carrying information on the Carrier of the Load, the assigned Dock where loading is executed, departure and required staging times. Thus, the Route is a concrete instance of an Itinerary.
If required, all stops of the Itinerary can be hit during the Route, which can be planned by an external Route Planning System. The shipping processes can be executed without Route management, only by handling Loads.
The possible lifecycle of Routes is described in the related description of the status values.

Loads

A Load comprises all Dispatch Load Units shipped together, usually in one truck or any other loading equipment. A Load can be assigned directly to a Dock if Routes are not used in the system. Otherwise, the Load is assigned to a Route.
All Dispatch Load Units, to be shipped together as a unit, are grouped to a Dispatch. A Dispatch is the part of the Load that represents for example the recipient of the goods or a single unloading point of the Route.
The possible lifecycle of Loads and Dispatches is described in the related description of the status values for Loads and Dispatches.

Marshalling and Loading

Dispatch Load Units that are ready for shipment are staged, after a possible intermediate storage, at a special Marshalling Grid located in the shipping area where the Dispatch Load Units could be marshaled in a required sequence.
Subsequent loading into a truck or other loading equipment occurs at the Dock assigned to the Marshalling Grid.
The loading process is done using RF-Terminal Loading and finished with closing the Load, printing shipping documents, and confirming truck departure which releases the occupied Dock for further use.

Shipping Topics

Basic functions and responsibilities of the Shipping subsystem:

  • Global general Lifecycle Management of Routes and Loads
  • Shipment of Orders (marshalling and loading) with providing the appropriate specific execution management
  • Administration of special Marshalling Areas
  • Administration of Shipping Areas including Docks.
