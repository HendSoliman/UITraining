Receiving

Overview

Receiving provides the components responsible for managing goods receiving in a warehouse. There are different ways to create inventory within the Dematic iQÂ®:

  • Inventory is created within a receiving process covered by Receiving based on
    • Advices
    • Shipments of Purchase Orders
  • Inventory is created within a correction booking (e.g. caused by unexpected Inventory or Stocktaking). This is not covered here.

A warehouse can receive internal or external deliveries. Goods may come from different sources, such as suppliers or production. Or they may be a return. An external delivery is usually due to a Purchase Order. Purchase Order management and booking a PO against the internal delivery is an ERP System task (host system). Dematic iQ® only provides an overview of the existing Purchase Orders. An internal delivery is usually due to a Production Order or similar.

The operational receiving process is fulfilled by a user at a Receiving Workstation or automatically with a telegram from the underlying conveyor control (e.g. with goods from a production site). Receiving in a Receiving Area is also possible using an RF-Terminal.

Advice based Receiving

The host system creates an Advice for the delivered goods after an acknowledgement of the goods receipt to the host system (external goods receipt of the supplier or internal goods receipt from production). Advices are transmitted automatically to the Dematic iQ® using the host Interface.

Receiving based on Deliveries

The receiving process could be executed based on single Deliveries of a Shipment which could be assigned to Purchase Orders (or Advices as well). Purchase Orders and Deliveries are transmitted automatically to the Dematic iQ® as well.

 INFO 

Goods receipts without an provided Advice or Delivery are not supported by Dematic iQ®. In case of handling manually unplanned goods receipts an Advice must be created.

Basic functions and responsibilities of the Receiving subsystem:

  • Management of Advices, Advice Lines, Advice Line Load Units.
  • Management of Purchase Orders, Purchase Order Lines, Shipments, Deliveries, Receipts and Receipt Load Units.
  • Management of Full Load Unit Receiving processes (receiving of Full Load Units from production and from a Supplier) including Inventory and Load Unit creation.
  • Management of Quantity Based Receiving processes in automated or manual Areas - Receiving Workstation or RF-Terminals - with deposition of single parts onto different Load Units.

Description

Introduction to Shipment Administration

Description of Shipments and Purchase Orders.

Purchase Orders

A Purchase Order is an order that requests the shipment of goods from a supplier. A Purchase Order can be delivered in one or multiple Shipments.

Shipments

The screen is used to administrate the Shipments and Shipment Claims and allows for register new Shipments and assign Deliveries and Docks to a Shipment.

Deliveries

The screen is used to administrate the Deliveries and related Receipts, view the assigned Receipt Load Units and allows for changing the not yet completed Deliveries.

Receipt Overview

This screen shows all Receipts joining Shipments, Deliveries and Receipt Load Units.

Shipment Claim Types

A Claim Type classifies the claim reason and assigns a configuration for processing.

Introduction to Advice Administration

Description of Advices.

Advices

Administration of Advices, Advice Lines and Advice Line Load Units.

Advices (Archive)

In the Advice Administration (Archive) screens only corresponding archived data are considered.

Advice Overview

The Advice Overview displays Advice Lines and all already assigned Load Units of an Advice Line.

Quick Advice Creation

With the manual "quick & easy" Advice creation the Advice is completed in a single step, with minimal user input, and is released immediately. It can consist of one or multiple Advice Lines.

Quantity based Receiving

Provides all functionality for deposit of delivered Inventory in single parts onto Load Units and initiates concluding inbound transports in automated or manual Areas at Receiving Workstations or with support of RF-Terminals ordered to given Advices or Deliveries.

Full Load Unit Receiving

Describes Full Load Unit Receiving in automated or manual Areas at a Receiving Workstation. Provides all functionality for Receiving fully advised Load Units and initiating concluding inbound transports.
