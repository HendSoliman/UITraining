Spontaneous Stacking and de-stacking
of Load Units

The Restacking screen supports spontaneous stacking of whole Load Units onto other Load Units and De-stacking. After a Source Load Unit scan, all stacked Load Units on this Load Unit display. With a Destination Load Unit scan, the subsequent booking destination is defined. The Load Unit or Insert is booked onto the Destination Load Unit or directly on the Workstation's location.

Spontaneous Repacking of Inventory

Repacking screen supports spontaneous unpacking of Inventory Units onto other Load Units or Inserts. After Scan of Source Load Unit relevant data of all Inventory Units on it will be displayed respectively selectable. With scan of Destination Load Unit the destination of subsequent booking is defined. After selecting the desired Inventory Unit, entering the quantity and executing the "Unpack" function, the entered quantity of the selected Inventory Unit is booked onto the Destination Load Unit or selected Insert. Based on the configuration, the Load Units automatically receives an inbound transport.

Spontaneous Stacking and de-stacking
of Load Units using a RF-Terminal

Spontaneous "Stacking" may be performed using an RF-Terminal Stacking. By Scanning Source and Target Load Units, they are stacked or de-stacked accordingly.
